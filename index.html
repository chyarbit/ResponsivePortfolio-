About Me

Technology is tricky for some people- there's so many cool things out there, but it's really hard for people without the background to understand what is so cool about the tech or even what the technology is. That's where I come in. With 10 years of government administration under my belt and my full-stack development certification, I am able to bridge that gap for you. I have the technical background to understand what is going on from the eyes of a developer and the experience of being on the non-technical side trouble shooting the front end from the user experience. With both of these tools in my pocket, I am able to communicate the cool technology that needs to be done in plain language to those that might not have that background. To put it simply, I make tech sound cool to users and get them excited to use the product!
